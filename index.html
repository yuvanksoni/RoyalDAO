About RoyalDAO

Who We Are

RoyalDAO is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) uniting Rajasthan's Web3 ecosystem – founders, developers, devrels, marketers, and Web3 enthusiasts committed to shaping the future of the decentralized internet.

Why We Exist

Rajasthan is home to brilliant builders and passionate learners, but there's a lack of local Web3 infrastructure. RoyalDAO exists to:

  • Foster deeper connections in the Web3 space
  • Enable collaboration across domains
  • Support onboarding for Web3 newbies
  • Make Jaipur a recognized Web3 innovation hub

What We Do
